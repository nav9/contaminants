How to Use the Visualization

×

Graph Interaction

  • Left Click Node: View detailed information in the sidebar.
  • Right Click Node: Open a menu to Toggle Glow (manual highlight) or Pin Node (fix position).
  • Drag Node: Move a node around. If pinned, it will stay where you drop it.

Navigation

  • Scroll Wheel: Zoom in and out of the graph.
  • Left Click & Drag (Empty Space): Pan around the graph.
  • Controls (Bottom Left): Use the and buttons to zoom, and the button to reset the view to fit all nodes.

Search & Tags

  • Search for contaminants, foods, or diseases to highlight them.
  • The (Bulb Icon) clears all active highlights and tags.
